Canary's Art Gallery founded in 1890 after the death of Vincent Van Gogh. Canary's Art Gallery represents the best of the art that tells a story and requires thought in-order to understand the meaning of Art. Our Gallery features pieces that can be worth upto 23.7 million dollars. Canary's Art Gallery proudly displays and helps find these paintings a better home. The paintings are kept in top original quality by our experts at Canary's Art Gallery.

Join us for our exhibition on April 19th at St Clair College for our in-person sale. You can also purchase online through our product portal.
